Where the Money Goes

The Austin C. Machitar Scholarship is a character-based award — not based on academic achievement — for a graduating senior in the Academy of Criminology and Justice (AOCJ) at Otay Ranch High School who brings positivity, connection, and joy to those around them.

The Austin C. Machitar Scholarship

Selection is merit-based and anonymous. Applications will be accepted for the 2026 to 2027 school year.
